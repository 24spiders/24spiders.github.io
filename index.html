About Me

Research Associate, Spatial Modeling, Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Alberta. My work focuses on developing practical applications of machine learning and data science methods for remote sensing data (drone, aerial, and satellite) to solve geospatial problems and enhance decision-making, with an emphasis on forestry and wildfire science.
